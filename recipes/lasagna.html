Homemade Lasagna
Classic Lasagna using bechamel instead of cheese, mostly popularized in america

  • Pasta for Lasagna
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Minced meat
  • Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Herbs like oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mozarella
  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Brown minced meat
  3. Add herbs to minced meat
  4. Add tomato sauce to minced meat
  5. Make Bechamel Sauce
  6. Drain the noodles
  7. Preheat the oven
  8. Assemble the lasagna starting with pasta->sauce->minced meat
