Protein Oats
Cooked oatmeal with addition of whey powder as well as sweeteners

  • Oatmeal
  • Salt
  • Whey Powder
  • Fruit
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Cocoa powder
  1. Add Oatmeal to boiling water
  2. Cook till water evaporates
  3. Add whey powder
  4. Add cinnamon powder
  5. Add cocoa powder
  6. Add salt
  7. Mix
  8. Add fruit on top
