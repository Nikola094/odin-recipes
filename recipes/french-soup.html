Frech Onion Soup
Classic French Onion Soup relying on caramelized onions and beef stock

  • Onions
  • Beef Bouyon
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Thyme or similar herbs
  • Baguette slices
  • Parmesan
  1. Add oil to pan
  2. Add chopped onions to pan
  3. Add sugar to onions
  4. Cook till caramenlized
  5. Deglaze the pan
  6. Add stock and herbs
  7. Cook till tastes combine
  8. Add baguette with parmesan on top
  9. Broil baguette and cheese
